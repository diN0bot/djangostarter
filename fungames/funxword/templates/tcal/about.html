Purpose

We want to make hanging out with friends as easy as possible. To do this we figure out what you and the people around you are up to, and then display that in an easy to read calendar. No fluff, no hassle, just here's what's going on this week.

What do you think? Could this be useful to you or someone you know? Is there something else you're looking for?

Our efforts are currently focused on extracting this information from twitter updates. If you're interesting in working with us as a developer or beta tester please let us know.
