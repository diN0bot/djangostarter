Feel Good About your Schedule

How often do you... watch tv or video online? Read the news? Play games? Chat?

Keep track of how you spend your time and feel good about being online.

ProcrasDonate?

ProcrasDonation is how to make your spare time count. Give pennies to your favorite causes for every hour you spend goofing around on selected websites.

Support Quality Content

You can also mark sites as "Time Well Spent" and donate to them directly. (Coming Soon!)

Progress, Goals, and Budget

So what's your time worth?

How many pennies will you pledge per hour of ProcrasDonation? Weekly scheduling goals help keep track of your progress and spending limits let you set a budget.

Your Records are Private

Your individual ProcrasDonation records are kept on your computer and are not accessible by anyone else. We could not even access them in response to a court order.

Summary information that your browser shares with our community is anonymous.

You may select "Announce my Donations on Twitter" (Coming Soon). Doing so will make the amounts of your donations and recipients publicly available.

How It Works

Add our free software to your web browser and set your preferences.

Pledges will automatically be made on your behalf based on your preferences.

Turning Pledges into Donations

Pledges are linked to your Twitter username. Use Tipjoy to approve your pledges. We'll send you occasional emails to remind you of any unpaid balance.

Why We Made ProcrasDonate

We're the same people that brought you Buy It Like You Mean It. That's why bilumi is prelisted when you choose your favorite charities.

We started ProcrasDonate because traditional grant funding is unprepared to support innovative projects like bilumi and quality online journalism. ProcrasDonate is a flexible, user-centered funding source for charities and content providers.
