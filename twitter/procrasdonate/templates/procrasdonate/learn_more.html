ProcrasDonate?

ProcrasDonation is how to make your spare time count. Give pennies to your favorite causes for every hour you spend goofing around on selected websites.

Support Quality Content

You can also support sites marked as "Time Well Spent" with hourly donations. (Coming Soon!)

Feel Good About your Schedule

How much time do you spend watching tv online? Reading news? Playing games? Chatting?

Keep track of your time and feel good about spending time online.

Customization

So what's your time worth? How many pennies will you donate for every hour you ProcrasDonate? Weekly scheduling goals help keep track of your progress and spending limits let you set a budget.

Your Records are Private

Your individual ProcrasDonation records are kept on your computer and are not accessible by anyone else. We could not even access them in response to a court order.

Summary information that your browser shares with our community is anonymous.

You may select "Announce my Donations on Twitter" (Coming Soon). Doing so will make the amounts of your donations and recipients publicly available.

How Does It Work?

Users like you add our free software to your web browser and then answer some questions about your preferences. You'll be able to track how you spend your time online set goals for yourself.

Pledges will automatically be made on your behalf to the organizations of your choice.

How Do I Pay?

Pledges are linked to your Twitter username. Use Tipjoy to approve your pledges and pay your account balance. Don't worry, we'll send you occasional emails to remind you of any unpaid balance.

About Us

We're the same people that brought you Buy It Like You Mean It. That's why bilumi is prelisted when you choose your favorite charities.

We started ProcrasDonate because traditional grant funding is not equipped to support innovative projects like bilumi. It is our hope that ProcrasDonate represents a more flexible funding paradigm for charities and struggling content providers.
