What is this?

This site is part of a twitter application that udpates users's twitter homepage backgrounds to reflect their local weather.

Screenshot

How does it work?

Use this site to select a weather theme. You will also need to authorize this application to update your twitter background on behalf of you.

Every hour, a script checks the Yahoo! Weather RSS Feed for your local weather report. From the theme you selected, the script chooses the image that best matches the current weather conditions. Finally, the script uses the Twitter REST Apito update your twitter homepage's background.
