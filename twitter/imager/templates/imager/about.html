Backgrounds are fun!

A few months ago, Google labs launched Gmail Themes.

When I saw this, I selected a peaceful-looking theme called "Tree". Curiously, that selection required that I enter my location.

As the days went by I discovered my gmail background changed according to the weather. When it rained, gmail became overcast. Water droplets glistened on the workspace bar.

There was something joyful and whimsical about discovering new backgrounds as the weather changed.

With Weatherizer, I hope to bring that joy to Twitter.

The more the merrier

More is better. More themes, more backgrounds per theme, more variations, more sharing.

Weatherization encourages anyone to create and share themes. Themes can have as few as 6 or as many as 47 backgrounds or icons, and may eventually incorporate temperature and time of day information.

Discover a new theme and share your experience.
